

Scope of the Application

The application is designed for the following environments and stakeholders:

- Target Institution: EKC Genius Sdn. Bhd. (EKC Genius Academy).
- Primary Subjects: Children in early childhood education centers and special education programs, specifically those with autism.

    
User Roles:

- Administrators: Focused on overall operations, including teacher and student management.
- Teachers: Focused on recording assessments, managing classroom activities, and monitoring student progress.
- Parents: Focused on remote monitoring of their child's progress and viewing financial records.

    
Project Duration: 12 months.

2. Problem Statement

The document identifies several critical issues currently faced by the education center:

- Manual Processes: Monitoring student development in early education and autism programs is largely manual or non-integrated, making systematic tracking difficult.

- Lack of Structured Analysis: There is no data analysis system to identify specific strengths and weaknesses in a student's development.

- Identification Hurdles: It is difficult to identify students who require early intervention, particularly in special education.

- Communication Gaps: Developmental information is difficult to share systematically with parents.

- Disjointed Operations: Management of attendance, activities, and basic records is handled separately rather than in one unified system.


3. Key Features

The application integrates IR4.0 technologies, mathematical analytics, and AI to provide the following features:
Assessment & Analytics

- Digital Evaluation System: A centralized database for teachers to record student assessments using a 1–5 scale rubric.

- AI-Driven Development Analysis: Uses mathematical models to process data and identify learning patterns or the need for early intervention.

- Early Warning Indicators: The system provides automated alerts ("Early Warning") when a student's development indicators fall below a certain threshold (e.g., a score below 2.5).

- Predictive Analysis: A feature to calculate a "Predicted Score" based on current scores and average growth rates.

Visualization & Reporting

- Radar/Spiderweb Charts: Interactive visual reports that allow users to compare a student's current performance against previous data across multiple indicators (e.g., communication, social interaction, and cognitive development).

- Automated Digital Reports: Generates development reports automatically for parents, reducing preparation time from hours to minutes.

- AI Chatbot: An integrated assistant that uses natural language to help users interpret time-series data, trends, and anomalies.

Operational Management

- Integrated Operations Module: Consolidates attendance tracking, activity management, and student/teacher records into one platform.

- Payment Management: Includes a system for tracking payments, viewing history, and generating monthly invoices for parents.
