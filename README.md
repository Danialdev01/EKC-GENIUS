# EKC Genius IR4.0 Platform

An integrated **IR4.0 platform** leveraging **Mathematical Analytics** and **Artificial Intelligence** to modernize management, intervention, and inclusive education for early childhood centers, specifically for students with autism.

---

## 📌 Overview
Developed in collaboration between **Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI)** and **EKC Genius Sdn. Bhd.**, this system transitions manual educational processes into a digital ecosystem. It focuses on identifying student strengths and weaknesses through data-driven insights to provide timely early interventions.

## ⚠️ Problem Statement
*   **Manual Records:** Learning progress and operations are currently recorded manually, leading to inefficiency and data silos.
*   **Unstructured Data:** Lack of analytical systems to identify specific developmental patterns or objective strengths and weaknesses.
*   **Delayed Intervention:** Difficulty in spotting students who need immediate special education support or early intervention.
*   **Communication Gaps:** Systematic reporting to parents is difficult to manage, often resulting in delayed updates.
*   **Disjointed Operations:** Management of attendance, activities, and basic center records is handled separately rather than in a unified system.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Smart Assessment & AI Analytics
*   **Digital Rubrics:** Teachers record assessments on a scale of 1–5 across critical indicators such as communication, social interaction, and cognitive development.
*   **Early Warning System:** Automated AI alerts and intervention suggestions when a student's score falls below critical thresholds (e.g., < 2.5).
*   **Predictive Analysis:** Forecasts future student development based on current scores and average growth rates to help plan long-term educational goals.

### 2. Advanced Visualization
*   **Spiderweb/Radar Charts:** Interactive visual profiles comparing current performance against previous assessments to highlight progress at a glance.
*   **Trend Analysis:** Visual tracking of developmental patterns, categorizing progress as "Improvement," "Stable," or "Declining."

### 3. AI Chatbot Assistant
*   A natural language assistant trained to help users interpret complex time-series data, identify anomalies, and explain predicted trends in plain language.

### 4. Operational Management
*   **Admin View:** Centralized management for student/teacher records, attendance tracking, and payment processing.
*   **Teacher View:** Focused on classroom activities, digital assessments, and receiving AI-driven recommendations for student care.
*   **Parent View:** Remote progress monitoring, automated digital reports, and billing/invoice history.

---

## 🧮 Mathematical Analytics Foundation
The system utilizes core mathematical elements to ensure data accuracy and objective reporting:

*   **Growth Rate:** Measures the speed of a student's progress:
    $$Growth\ Rate = \frac{Score_{final} - Score_{initial}}{Score_{initial}} \times 100$$
*   **Predictive Score:** Estimates future outcomes based on the current trajectory:
    $$Predicted\ Score = Current\ Score + Average\ Growth$$
*   **Development Index:** Calculates monthly and annual averages to determine long-term trends and center-wide performance.
